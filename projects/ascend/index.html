Ascend

Published on: 2014-02-10

It's about three workers cooperating together whith their abilities to move a platform, which allows them to go higher and higher. You control characters with arrow keys and you can jump with space bar. You change over which character you control with buttons 1 to 3.

Programmers: Dimitri Belopopsky (ShadowMitia) and Etienne Sebille (zigzag)

Lead Artist and Sound Engineer: Pierre Lecouvey (Peggy)

Game Design and Level Design: Guillaume Salinaro (Captainhum)

Thanks to Global Game Jam for giving us a wonderful time, and magnificient opportunity to challenge ourselves. Thanks to APOIL for hosting the event and supply the space and the food!
